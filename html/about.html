About Us

/SERE/

Sere is a Taita word for peace. The name encapsulates everything we stand for: peace, scents, self-care, self-love, serenity
Here at SERE, we believe that when you and your home smell good, you feel good and when you feel good you're literally unstoppable... and that's the ultimate self-care

Our History

Started by two sisters, Sere is a company that was built in 2021 to encourage self care and self love.

Our Mission

We are intent on giving people a chance to be at ease and let off the worries of this world. All our products and services are designed to help our clients relax and be comfortable.

Our Vision

We envision ourselves growing to become the go-to store for self-care products such as home fragrance and body care products. We also will give a platform for conversations centered on self care and self love. This will create a community of like-minded individuals who treasure therapeautic products and conversations.
